01. Data Collection

At Bijaybizsolutions, we collect only the essential data required to facilitate your business formation in the USA and UK. This includes legal names, contact information, and necessary identification documents for government filings.

02. Security Standards

Your data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. We do not store sensitive payment information on our servers; all transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant partners.

03. Third-Party Disclosure

Information is shared only with relevant government authorities (IRS, Companies House, etc.) and registered agents strictly for the purpose of company formation. We never sell your data to third-party marketers.

04. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting our support team at support@bijaybizsolutions.com.
